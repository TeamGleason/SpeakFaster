How to abbreviate a phrase

  • Maximum allowed length of abbreviation: {{maxAbbreviationLength}} characters
  • All characters should be lowercase
  • Omit all sentence-final punctuation
    • How are you? → hay
  • Keep sentence-middle punctuation, without space
    • The more you practice, the better you will get → tmyp,tbywg
  • Keep Arabic numerals, without space
    • See you at 9 → sya9
  • For contractions, omit and split at the apostrophe and take the initial letters
    • You won't believe this → ywtbt
    • I'll tell you about it later → iltyail
    • If there is no contraction, simply use the first letter of each word. I do not know. → idnk
